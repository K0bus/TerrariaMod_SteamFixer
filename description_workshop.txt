Tired of losing your hard-earned tModLoader achievements when switching back to Steam Terraria? This mod seamlessly imports your tModLoader achievements into your Steam Terraria profile, allowing you to continue your progress without missing a single milestone.

[h3]Features:[/h3]

Automatically imports all compatible tModLoader achievements.
Maintains your progress across Steam Terraria and tModLoader.
Simple installation and easy to use.
Lightweight and efficient, with no performance impact.

[h3]Note:[/h3]

Only compatible with the latest version of Terraria and tModLoader.
Backup your save files before use to ensure your progress remains safe.
Take your Terraria experience to the next level by bringing all your tModLoader achievements into Steam Terraria!
Be patient, Terraria achievements will be loaded once you close the game.
